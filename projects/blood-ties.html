Blood Ties

ROLE:: Game Designer
TIMELINE:: Jan 2025 - Mar 2025
ENGINE:: Unreal Engine
GENRE:: First-Person Action

Mission Briefing / Overview

Story-driven FPS where the player must rescue their kidnapped brother from a gang. The game features a progressive narrative twist where the brother ultimately becomes the final boss. My focus was on executing tight combat mechanics, challenging encounter design, and deliberate pacing that aligns with the story's rising tension.

Core Mechanics

  • Visceral Gunplay: Designed responsive feedback loops to make every interaction feel impactful. Heavy focus on hitscan validation and audio-visual punch.
  • Dynamic Encounters: Level layouts emphasize verticality and flanking, encouraging players to move aggressively rather than playing defensively.
  • Narrative Pacing: Used environmental storytelling and enemy placements to foreshadow the final confrontation without explicit cutscenes.

Level Design Philosophy

The levels in Blood Ties are designed as "combat bowls". Each arena has clear entry and exit points, with central structures creating organic cover. Ammo and health pickups are strategically placed to naturally guide the player through the intended golden path during firefights.
