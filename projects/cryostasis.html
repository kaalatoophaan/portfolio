Mission Briefing / Overview

The player awakens from a cryopod in a ruined space station shrouded in darkness. Core gameplay revolves around deliberate exploration, strict resource management, and escalating tension. There is a strong focus on atmosphere, level flow, and environmental storytelling.

Design Process & Mechanics

  • Resource Scarcity: Tuned the drop rates of essential items like flashlight batteries and oxygen reserves to maintain a high stress level without unfair punishment.
  • Environmental Storytelling: Crafted the station's decay to tell the story of its downfall. Used lighting cues (or lack thereof) to naturally unnerve the player.

Level Flow Architecture

Map layouts were explicitly designed to force players out of their comfort zone. Backtracking serves a narrative purpose, introducing new hazards to previously 'safe' zones. Navigating the station feels confusing at first, mirroring the protagonist's amnesia.
