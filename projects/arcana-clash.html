GENRE:: Speed & Strategy Card Game

Mission Briefing / Overview

Arcana Clash is "The Ultimate Speed & Strategy Card Game." Designed from the ground up to combine quick-reflex gameplay with deep arcanum synergies. Players compete to outpace and outwit opponents using complex deck mechanics.

Core Mechanics

  • Paced Card Play: Cards must be dealt and activated physically or digitally with split-second timing.
  • Synergy Engine: Building strategic decks that string together magical elements to achieve burst conditions.
  • Competitive Meta: Rigorously balanced rulesets ensuring no single deck archetype dominates the arena.

Design Philosophy

The goal was to bridge the gap between traditional slower-paced trading card games and high-velocity tabletop experiences. Creating physical interaction rules and balancing the core economy of the cards was the central design challenge.
